How AI is advancing education in today's society.

Learning in the classroom comes with many challenges. With the world in covid-19 lockdown, Most students have continued their learning from home. A natural advantage AI has is it is being used to today with online learning and can be accessed anywhere with an internet connection. With all learning being online, AI has helped teachers identify student difficulties where in a traditional classroom be un-noticeable and neglected. AI also is efficient and personalized to each individual student, this has allowed students to understand the content aswell as understand concepts in the right order. AI is also benefiting teachers. AI can automate redundant and painstaking administrative tasks for the teachers.
